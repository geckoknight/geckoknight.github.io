A few years from now, the world may look quite different: A world …

with vast ruins and no human left on the planet.

But in such a world, there will be beauty.

And chance may have it that it will still be brimming with life.

Some over the ground, and some under it.

Who would have thought that what we may experience as the end …

… could be considered as a beginning.

And in such a world plays our little story.

Deep down under the vines of the forest, …

… there lived a gecko.

It never really left its home but was always curious about life outside.

One day, it decided to search for food outside its normal territory.

As the gecko wandered the depths of an unknown cave, …

… it came near the tangling roots of a tree, …

… into a greener area.

The gecko saw vines hanging down from the ceiling.

And it saw the light of the surface, a bright blue tone that seemed new.

Stepping outside the cave for the first time …

… was a whole new experience.

It wandered the strange landscape, and when night approached …

The gecko found a safe place below vines with tasty fireflys, …

where it gazed towards the sky, hardly able to keep the eyes open.

There seemed to be a nice spot covered in light, …

the gecko experienced as it drifted into the dream world.

The sun was slowly rising as the gecko opened its eyes.

It climbed a mountain to get a better overview of the surroundings.

Only to find out that this area …

… was next to the ocean.

The gecko headed even higher up the mountain …

… and saw an amazing rock structure.

While approaching, the gecko asked itself: Was it inhabited?

Different kinds of rocks and noone to be seen.

Strange seemed this structure.

Dark and sinister, not as inviting from the inside as it seemed from

And then, a dead end: Water.

The gecko headed back but got lost in this maze-like structure.

One path after the other he took, …

… until he saw a bright light.

Finally: the surface.

The gecko seemed trapped between huge stony walls.

But it found the way to the sky again.

What was this place? And where were all the trees and plants?

And as the sun set, the gecko searched for shelter.

Unable to climb another hill, the gecko just looked for another hole to hide

There was something like an entrance.

Strangely illuminated by fireflies, the gecko rested in this cavern.

Dreaming of another place to see, he fell into a deep sleep.

When he woke up, the sun filled the cavern with light.

Again, the gecko travelled the whole day.

The gecko traversed the empty desert.

And came across another strange structure.

As if they were from another life time.

Errected by a different species, …

… but now left in ruins and abandoned.

But something was new here: There were objects lying around.

Then, the night approached and it was getting dark.

And as the sun set, the gecko recognized towers on the horizon.

He hid between some stones because there was nothing else around.

When the sun was rising the next morning, the gecko discovered trees.

On the other side of the hill was a jungle, plentiful plants, full with life.

Under the crowns of the trees, it was hard to keep track of time.

The gecko found its way through the jungle.

A splendid meal awaited the gecko around every corner…

And the jungle was inhabited by other lizards.

A small settlement where the gecko could ask around and relax.

And when the end of the day was near, …

The gecko asked for shelter and slept peacefully.

The next morning, the sun rose over the trees.

A local cave prove to be full of plants and vines – and food.

There was so much to see, but it was also easy to get lost in the vast

Traversing the the vegetation, the gecko found himself in another place.

And as night approached, he found shelter below the thicket.

The next morning, the gecko was greeted by mushroom creatures.

They showed the gecko around, and it seemed that this part of the jungle …

Was unlike anything the gecko could imagine.

The mushroom creatures couldn't speak to the gecko.

But they were friendly and inviting, kind and patient.
